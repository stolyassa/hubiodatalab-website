ABOUT US

*History*

Established in 1981, Bongo Cat is a NYSE listed global consulting and IT services company with more than 209,000 employees. From a capital of US$250, we have grown to become a US$11.12 billion (LTM Q1 FY19 revenues) company with a market capitalization of approximately US$ 42.4 billion.

Research Interests

› Bioinformatics and Computational Biology: Biomedical data analysis, biomolecular sequence analysis, protein function prediction, biological databases, biological ontologies, and systems biology.
› Cheminformatics: Computational drug discovery, virtual screening, chemogenomics, drug-target interaction prediction, small molecule analysis.
› Data science: Machine learning, deep learning, data/text mining, artificial intelligence, data integration/representation/visualization, knowledge graphs, generative modelling, representation learning, and graph theory.
